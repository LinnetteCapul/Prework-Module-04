SEVENTEEN Wanna Rock With You!

Biography

Seventeen is a Korean pop idol group from South Korea that was formed in 2015 by Pledis Entertainment.

This idol group consists of 13 members that is divided into three sub-unit groups: Hip-Hop Team, Vocal Team, and Performance Team.

Their name originates from having 13 members, 3 sub-units, and the 1 group as a whole which adds up to 17.

Because there are so many members, they are known to have the one of the most difficult fanchants: reciting each member's name between lyrics in certain songs.

The offical fan club name is "Carat". Fans voted on their name back in 2016 and originated from a song called "Shining Diamonds".

The group had performed this song before their debut and acknowledges their fans as diamonds, thus came to be the fan name: Carat. The offical fan club colors are rose quartz and serenity, which are pink and blue hues.
